Our Table

Cooked the way it is cooked at home.

Gealie is a family kitchen first and a restaurant second. The seasoning is built up over hours, not shaken from a jar. The thyme, the scotch bonnet, the pimento, the patience. You taste all of it.

Regulars walk in off Main Street, call out their order, and leave with a foil pan still warm in their hands. No fuss, no airs. Just generous portions of food that knows where it comes from.

“Tastes like home.” That is the whole goal.

The Jerk

Smoke does the work the spice can’t.

Real jerk is not a sauce poured on at the end. It is a wet rub of scotch bonnet, thyme, scallion and pimento that sits on the meat, then cooks low over coals until the char and the smoke meet in the middle.

That is why a Gealie jerk plate hits different from the steam-table version down the way. It is patience you can taste.
